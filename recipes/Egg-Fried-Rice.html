Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a popular Chinese dish made with cooked rice, scrambled eggs and often served hot. It is a versatile and flavourful meal that is easy to customize with your favourite ingriedients.

Ingriedients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (prefreably a day old)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots (optional)
  • 1/4 cup green peas (optional)
  • 2-3 spring onions (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable/sesame oil)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook and cool the rice (leftover rice works best).
  2. Heat some oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Take them out and keep aside.
  3. In the same pan, add a little more oil and saut√© chopped garlic and onions.
  4. Add chopped vegetables like carrots and peas if you like, and stir-fry for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice and stir well to mix everything.
  6. Pour in some soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix it all together.
  7. Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and mix.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
